## Tracked International Rankings

### Times Higher Education (THE)
A global ranking built on five pillars: teaching, research environment, research quality, citations, international outlook, and industry income.

### QS World University Rankings
Published annually by the UK-based Quacquarelli Symonds, weighing academic reputation, employer reputation, faculty-to-student ratio, and citations per faculty member.

### Scimago Institutions Rankings
Measures research performance, innovation, and societal impact of academic and research institutions.

### Webometrics
Issued by the Spanish National Research Council; based on the university's web presence and the online impact of its publications.

### Round University Ranking (RUR)
Assesses institutional performance through 20 indicators distributed across four main areas.

### Nature Index
Built on the count of papers published in a selected set of high-quality scientific journals chosen by an international panel.

> **Note:** Al-Qadisiyah University's data in each ranking is updated periodically as new annual editions are released. For the latest results contact the department at: qu.assurance@qu.edu.iq
