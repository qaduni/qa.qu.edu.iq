## Tracked Arab Rankings

### QS Arab Region University Rankings
A regional edition of the QS ranking, focused on universities in Arab countries; criteria include academic reputation, employer reputation, faculty-to-student ratio, research, and internationalization.

### 4ICU Arab Region
A web-based ranking produced by 4ICU that measures the web presence and online impact of Arab universities.

### Association of Arab Universities Ranking
A ranking issued by the Association of Arab Universities measuring the academic, research and social performance of member institutions.

### SCImago Arab Region
A regional edition of the Scimago ranking covering universities and research institutions in the Arab region.

---

> **Note:** University data is updated in each ranking after the annual edition is released.
