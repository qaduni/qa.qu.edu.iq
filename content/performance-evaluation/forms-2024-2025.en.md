## Forms for the 2024-2025 Academic Year

The 2024-2025 institutional performance-evaluation framework comprises an integrated set of forms directed at colleges, centers, and administrative departments.

## Form Types

### College Performance Form
Covering:
- Academic programmes and learning outcomes
- Faculty and staff
- Students and student services
- Research and creative activity
- Infrastructure and laboratories
- Community service and partnerships
- Internal quality assurance

### Research-Centre Performance Form
Covering:
- Research output
- Laboratories and equipment
- External collaboration
- Patents and innovation

### Administrative-Department Performance Form
Covering:
- Quality of services provided
- Turnaround times
- Beneficiary satisfaction
- Follow-up and complaints handling

## Implementation Instructions

1. Forms distributed at the start of the academic year
2. Implementation across the first and second semesters
3. Data aggregated before the year ends
4. Analysis and final report issued
