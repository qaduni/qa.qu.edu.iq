## Surveys

The department designs and administers a range of surveys directed at the various beneficiaries of university services, in order to measure satisfaction and gather feedback for performance improvement.

## Principal Surveys

### Faculty Evaluation Survey (Students)
Administered at the end of each semester to measure student satisfaction with teaching-staff performance; results feed into teaching-performance evaluation and professional-development plans.

### Programme Satisfaction Survey (Students)
Measures students' satisfaction with academic programmes in terms of content, teaching and assessment methods, and available resources.

### Graduate Survey
Administered to alumni some time after graduation to measure how well the programme prepared them for the labour market and professional needs.

### Employer Satisfaction Survey
Directed at employers of the university's graduates, measuring their satisfaction with graduate quality and skills.

### Workplace Satisfaction Survey (Staff)
Measures satisfaction of university faculty and administrative staff with the work environment, benefits, and opportunities available.

### Administrative Services Satisfaction Survey
Measures satisfaction of students and faculty with the quality of services provided by administrative departments.

## Implementation Approach

- Periodic administration on a defined schedule
- Use of electronic forms where possible
- Confidentiality of respondents
- Analysis of results and issuance of reports
- Submission of recommendations to relevant bodies
