## Institutional Performance-Evaluation Guidance Manual

The guidance manual serves as a reference document for colleges and centers when completing the institutional performance-evaluation forms. It aims to standardize concepts and ensure the quality of data submitted.

## Manual Contents

### Chapter One: Introduction
- Overview of the institutional performance-evaluation system
- General objectives
- Bodies responsible for implementation

### Chapter Two: Concepts and Terminology
- Definitions of the principal indicators
- Units of measurement
- Methodology for calculating ratios

### Chapter Three: Completion Instructions
- Steps for completing the form
- Required evidence and documentation
- Deadlines

### Chapter Four: Verification and Audit
- Internal review mechanism
- External audit
- Feedback loops

### Annexes
- Form templates
- Checklists
- Worked examples
- Indicator tables
