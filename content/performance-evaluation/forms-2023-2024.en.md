## Forms for the 2023-2024 Academic Year

Archive of the institutional performance-evaluation forms applied at Al-Qadisiyah University during the 2023-2024 academic year.

## Evaluation Pillars

### Academic Pillar
- Academic programmes and their updating
- Learning outcomes and their assessment
- Teaching and assessment methods
- Faculty research output

### Administrative Pillar
- Organizational structure
- Human-resources management
- Financial resources
- Administrative organization

### Student Pillar
- Student services
- Extracurricular activities
- Academic advising
- Programme graduates

### Infrastructure Pillar
- Lecture halls
- Laboratories
- Libraries
- Technology and e-systems

> **Note:** The annual report covering the 2023-2024 evaluation outcomes has been issued and is available to authorized university bodies.
