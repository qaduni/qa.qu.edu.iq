## ISO Certifications

International Organization for Standardization (ISO) certifications are among the most widely recognized quality standards worldwide. The Quality Assurance Department tracks the attainment and periodic renewal of ISO certifications relevant to the university's operations.

## Relevant Certifications

### ISO 9001 — Quality Management Systems
An international standard that defines the requirements for a quality management system in an organization seeking to deliver products or services that meet customer and regulatory requirements.

### ISO 21001 — Management Systems for Educational Organizations
A standard tailored to educational institutions, focused on educational outcomes and meeting the needs of learners and other stakeholders.

### ISO/IEC 17025 — Competence of Testing and Calibration Laboratories
An international standard for the competence of testing and calibration laboratories, used in laboratory accreditation.

### ISO 14001 — Environmental Management Systems
A standard specifying requirements for an effective environmental management system to reduce environmental impact.

### ISO 45001 — Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems
A standard specifying requirements for an occupational health and safety management system to protect workers.

### ISO 27001 — Information Security Management
A standard specifying requirements for an information-security management system to protect data.

## Workflow

The Quality Assurance Department liaises with ISO certification bodies, prepares the required documentation, trains relevant staff, and follows up on the periodic audits required to maintain the certifications.
