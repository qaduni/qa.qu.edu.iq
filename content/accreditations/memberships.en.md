## Memberships and Subscriptions

Al-Qadisiyah University is keen to join international bodies and unions concerned with quality and academic accreditation, given the value of exchanging expertise and keeping up with developments in the field.

## Principal Memberships

### Association of Arab Universities (AArU)
Active membership in a union of Arab universities that coordinates academic and research cooperation across Arab states.

### International Network for Quality Assurance Agencies in Higher Education (INQAAHE)
An international network of more than 300 quality-assurance agencies from around the world.

### Arab Network for Quality Assurance in Higher Education (ANQAHE)
A regional network specialized in higher-education quality assurance across Arab countries.

### International Association of Universities (IAU)
A global association that fosters dialogue among universities and strengthens international academic cooperation.

### Memberships in International Databases
- Scopus
- Web of Science (Clarivate)
- DOAJ
- DOAR
- Crossref

## Benefits of Membership

- Exchanging expertise with peer universities and bodies
- Participating in international conferences and workshops
- Access to the latest standards and best practices
- Strengthening the university's academic reputation
- Benefiting from training and qualification programmes
