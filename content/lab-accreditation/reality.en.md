## Laboratory Landscape at Al-Qadisiyah University

Al-Qadisiyah University hosts a wide network of scientific, teaching and research laboratories distributed across its colleges; they are a cornerstone of both the academic and the research mission.

## Laboratories by College

### College of Medicine
- Anatomy laboratories
- Physiology laboratories
- Biochemistry laboratories
- Microbiology laboratories
- Histology and pathology laboratories

### College of Pharmacy
- Pharmaceutical-chemistry laboratories
- Pharmaceutics laboratories
- Pharmacognosy laboratories
- Toxicology laboratories

### College of Engineering
- Mechanical laboratories
- Electrical and electronics laboratories
- Fluid and thermal laboratories
- Materials and structures laboratories

### College of Science
- Chemistry laboratories
- Physics laboratories
- Biology laboratories
- Earth-sciences laboratories

### College of Agriculture
- Soil and water laboratories
- Plant-production laboratories
- Animal-production laboratories
- Food-technology laboratories

### College of Veterinary Medicine
- Parasitology laboratories
- Animal-pathology laboratories
- Animal-production laboratories

### College of Computer Science and IT
- Programming laboratories
- Networks laboratories
- Artificial-intelligence laboratories

## Challenges and Opportunities

- Some equipment requires modernization
- Expanding accreditation to additional laboratories
- Strengthening inter-laboratory cooperation
- Building the capacity of technical staff
