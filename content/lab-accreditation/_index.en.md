# Laboratory Accreditation

The Accreditation Division within the Quality Assurance Department maintains the inventory of laboratories across all of the university's colleges, documents their capabilities, follows up the accreditation procedures in line with the international standard ISO/IEC 17025, and disseminates laboratory-safety standards.
