## Documents and Records

The Quality Assurance Department provides the reference documentation that colleges and laboratories need to complete laboratory accreditation procedures in line with the adopted international standards.

## Document Types

### Standards Documents
- ISO/IEC 17025:2017
- Implementation guidance manuals
- Detailed accreditation requirements

### Procedural Documents
- Laboratory quality manual
- Laboratory operating procedures
- Equipment calibration procedures
- Result-verification procedures

### Records
- Training records
- Maintenance records
- Test records
- Customer-complaint records
- Non-conformity records

### Forms
- Test-request forms
- Test-report forms
- Laboratory-evaluation forms
- Management-review forms

## References

- ISO/IEC 17025:2017 — General requirements for the competence of testing and calibration laboratories
- ILAC G19 — Modules in a Forensic Science Process
- ISO 15189:2022 — Medical laboratories
- Iraqi Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research guidance

> **To obtain documents:** contact the Accreditation Division in the department.
