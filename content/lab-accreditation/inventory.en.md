## Laboratory Inventory

The Quality Assurance Department maintains an up-to-date database of every laboratory across the colleges and centers of Al-Qadisiyah University, covering:

## Inventory Data

### Basic Information
- Laboratory name
- Owning college or department
- Area and location
- Laboratory supervisor
- Disciplines served

### Equipment
- List of major instruments
- Year of acquisition
- Operational status
- Maintenance schedule

### Capabilities
- Approved tests and analyses
- Services provided to the university
- Services provided to external parties
- Research outputs

## Inventory Objectives

- Maintain a comprehensive database to inform management decisions
- Identify needs and gaps
- Plan expansions and upgrades
- Facilitate inter-laboratory cooperation
- Prepare accreditation files

## Updating the Inventory

Inventory data is updated annually via dedicated forms circulated to the colleges; submissions are reviewed in the department before being formally adopted.
